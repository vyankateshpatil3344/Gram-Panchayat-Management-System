According to Census 2011 information, the location code or village code of Yevaluj village is 567233. Yevaluj village is located in Panhala tehsil of Kolhapur district in Maharashtra, India. It is situated 37km away from sub-district headquarter Panhala (tehsildar office) and 20km away from district headquarter Kolhapur. As per 2009 stats, Yavluj is the gram panchayat of Yevaluj village.

The total geographical area of the village is 870.11 hectares. Yevaluj has a total population of 6,119 people, out of which the male population is 3,229 while the female population is 2,890. The literacy rate of Yevaluj village is 69.85%, out of which 75.69% males and 63.32% females are literate. There are about 1,278 houses in Yevaluj village.
